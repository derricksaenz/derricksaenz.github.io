"brightnight" Night Photography Series


The “brightnight” photography series was an experiment I wanted to take with my new Nikon D3300 DSLR camera, and because at this time I was still learning in class, I wanted to be able to do everything. I am a huge fan of those astrophotography shots I see everywhere on social media, and I wanted to try to copy that in some way on my own.

While no, these don’t capture the sky full of stars and a slice of the milky way, these do give me such a great feeling when I look at them for their color. I am proud of these photos for showing me what is capable with the camera, and I know for a fact that I could not have taken these photos with a regular phone.
