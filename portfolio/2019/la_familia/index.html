"La Familia" Portrait Series

My church has been a part of my life ever since I was born. While the church itself has gone through various iterations and people, the core community has always remained the same. These people have always been very welcoming to everyone, increasingly wise, and immensely kind to me. The largest influence in my life has been the people around me. They helped me become the person I am today, so all I want to do is give something back.

Something I learned in university is that photography’s primary purpose is to take a moment and to take it out of time — immortalize it. That’s what I appreciate about portraits, it takes people as way they are right there in that moment.

I want to do whatever I can to say thank you. If this series does anything to show them my gratitude, then I am satisfied.
