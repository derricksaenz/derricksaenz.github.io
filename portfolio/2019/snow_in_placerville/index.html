"La Familia" Portrait Series

“Snow in Placerville” is a small series of the city of Placerville during a snow trip. This was the first time I actually was somewhere while it was snowing, and it was surreal. I enjoy visiting the snow, and I find there is a different stillness of the environment compared to the city, like San Jose or San Francisco. My objective was to capture that piece in the photos.
